Background

I believe that my passion for electronics started from a romanticism over robotics. I was so fascinated by the visuals of shows like Gundam, Transformers, and other 90s anime, from there I knew that I wanted to be at the fore front of innovation of technology. Once attending university, I changed my major. I was initally Biomedical Engineering because I thought I would be able to build prosthetics; however, I soon realized that I am just more interested in the assembly and power aspect of things so I changed my major to electrical engineering and have been happy ever since.
